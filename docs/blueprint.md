# **App Name**: Electric Doctor's

## Core Features:

- AI Diagnostic Assistant: Users capture and upload images, videos, or audio of electrical issues; Gemini 1.5's vision and audio tools provide an initial diagnosis and identify damaged, worn, or missing parts.
- Smart Service & Product Recommendations: Based on the AI diagnosis, the app uses generative AI as a tool to craft persuasive messages (informed by 'Chris Voss and Chase Hughes logic') to recommend specific products and electrician services, highlighting benefits and urgency.
- Electrician Marketplace: Customers submit service requests, which are intelligently matched with available, qualified, and nearby electricians. Electricians manage their availability and accept jobs via the platform.
- Secure Booking & Payment: Facilitate secure booking and payment processing for requested services directly within the app, ensuring a seamless transaction for customers and electricians. Collect holds of up to $2597.00 for appointment booking, charging for time investment on a customer's call.
- Real-time Electrician Tracking & Communication: After booking, customers can view their assigned electrician's real-time location on a map (integrating Google Maps) and communicate directly through an in-app chat for coordination.
- User & Electrician Profile Management: Both customers and electricians can create and manage their profiles, view job history, service offerings, ratings, and certifications.
- AI-Powered Predictive Maintenance Alerts: Using historical data and real-time sensor input (if available), the AI predicts potential electrical failures and sends proactive alerts to users, recommending inspections or repairs.
- Dynamic Pricing for Urgent Services: Implement a dynamic pricing model based on real-time demand, electrician availability, and urgency of the service request, maximizing revenue during peak times.
- Upsell Opportunities: The AI identifies opportunities to upsell related products or services based on the initial diagnostic findings (e.g., suggesting surge protection after identifying wiring issues).

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep black (#000000) for a professional and serious tone, reflecting the critical nature of electrical work.
- Secondary color: Light orange (#FFB347) to highlight key interactive elements, calls to action, and important information, ensuring visibility and urgency.
- Background color: Dark grey (#333333) to provide contrast and readability against the black and orange elements.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, neutral, and highly readable characteristics, conveying professionalism and clarity essential for a service-oriented application.
- Employ a consistent set of modern, minimalist line icons in light orange that clearly represent electrical components, service tools, navigation, and user actions. This reinforces the app's clean and efficient aesthetic.
- A clean, intuitive, and mobile-first layout prioritizing ease of use for both customers and electricians. Critical information like AI diagnostics, service requests, and electrician tracking will be prominently displayed with clear calls to action.
- Implement subtle and purposeful animations, such as smooth transitions between views, distinct loading indicators, and tactile feedback on interactive elements. This ensures a fluid and responsive user experience without unnecessary distraction.
